Company

Overview

Established in 1987, Sewa Printing Press is promoted by a group of highly committed, innovative and well experienced persons and managed by a team of young and energetic professionals. Built on four decades of extensive experience in the printing industry, Sewa has been successfully catering to the multifarious printing needs of clientele that include several of the foremost national and international agencies, projects, government organizations & non-government organizations (NGOs) and advertising agencies among others.

In step with the technology of our time, our press is adequately equipped with the most advance range of printing machinery. Coupled with the expertise of a team of skilled operators, we offer a host of multi-colour printing services – many of which were till recently unheard of in Nepal. Moreover, we ensure the use of the finest paper for all high-quality printing jobs.

Services

The Sewa objectives is to provide punctual, precise and perfectly executed printing services.

01 Pre Press

Our professionally trained graphic designers are computer artists who know how to use their creative abilities to improve the look of your printed projects.

  • Designing
  • Plate making (CTP)

02 Printing

Our press printing facility boasts a Sheet fed offset machines that offer flexibility, consistent quality with minimal turnaround time in execution. We have 3 unit of 4-color printing machine and 2 single color printing machine. So, we could print as per the need and quantity of job quickly and efficiently.

  • Hidelberg Speed master CS-92 ( 24 * 36 )
  • Hidelberg Print master HG ( 13 * 19 )
  • Adast Dominat 745 ( 19 * 25 )
  • Adast Dominant ( 19 * 25 )

03 Post Press

Post-press is the most important stage of any printing process. It is the most crucial part even though it takes place after actual printing. We offer a variety of post-press services that are required for commercial printed materials and packages.

  • Binding
  • Lamination - Pvc - Bopp - Pet / Pearl / Gold
  • Creasing
  • Hot Stamp Foiling
  • Spot Uv
  • Flood Uv And Aqua - Gloss And Matt
  • Window Patching
  • Embossing
  • Die Cutting
  • Flap and Lock Pasting
